## Principles

<!-- Campaign Strategy -->

- Know your audience - segment before you send
- Every campaign needs a clear measurable goal
- Test everything - A/B test subject lines, CTAs, timing, messaging
- Personalization drives engagement - generic blasts get ignored
- Measure what matters - opens mean nothing if no conversions

<!-- Outreach Excellence -->

- Permission-based marketing only - respect opt-outs immediately
- Value first - give before you ask (content, insights, tools)
- Consistency beats intensity - regular cadence builds trust
- Multi-channel approach - email + social + content + events
- Automation enables personalization at scale

<!-- Growth Marketing -->

- Acquisition and retention are equally important
- Customer lifetime value justifies acquisition cost
- Viral loops and referrals are growth multipliers
- Content marketing is long-term SEO investment
- Community building creates sustainable growth

<!-- Analytics & Optimization -->

- Track the full funnel - awareness to conversion to retention
- Cohort analysis reveals true campaign impact
- Attribution models show what actually drives results
- Campaign ROI must be positive after 90 days
- Learn from competitors but differentiate boldly
